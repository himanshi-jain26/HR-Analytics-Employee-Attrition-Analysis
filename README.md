# HR-Analytics-Employee-Attrition-Analysis
End-to-end HR Analytics project analyzing employee attrition, workforce demographics, employee satisfaction, and retention drivers using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify the key factors influencing workforce turnover and employee retention.

The objective is to understand employee demographics, compensation patterns, job characteristics, workplace satisfaction, and organizational factors that contribute to attrition. The analysis provides actionable insights to help organizations improve employee engagement, retention, and workforce planning.

## Key Insights

### Employee Attrition

- Overall attrition rate is approximately 16%.
- Sales department recorded the highest attrition rate (20.6%).
- Research & Development demonstrated the strongest retention.

### Job Roles

- Sales Representatives experienced the highest turnover (~40% attrition rate).
- Laboratory Technicians also showed elevated attrition levels.
- Senior and managerial positions exhibited stronger retention.

### Workplace Factors

- Employees working overtime were significantly more likely to leave.
- Most employees reported moderate-to-high job satisfaction.
- Work-life balance ratings were generally positive.

### Workforce Demographics

- The average employee age is 37 years.
- Most employees belong to the 30–40 age group.
- Male employees represent approximately 60% of the workforce.

### Compensation

- Sales employees receive the highest average monthly income.
- Monthly income increases significantly with job level.
- Higher job levels are associated with greater compensation and experience.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive overtime to improve employee retention.
- Implement targeted retention programs for Sales and high-risk job roles.
- Strengthen employee engagement initiatives for single employees and high-turnover groups.
- Improve career progression and development opportunities.
- Monitor employee satisfaction and work-life balance regularly.
